Filipino Spaghetti Recipe

History:

Filipino spaghetti was created in the late 19th century. It was requested by Americans who wanted dishes that reminded them of theirs homes. During World War II, the Philippines experienced a shortage of tomatoes. This means they also ran out of tomato-based ingredients, including ketchup, which was the most important condiment for Americans at the time. A woman named Maria Orosa created a way to create a similar substitute with bananas. One day, a man asked his Filipino staff to make spaghetti for him. They used the banana ketchup and replaced the meatballs with chopped up hot dogs.

This recipe will show you how to create tasty and delicious Filipino-style spaghetti. This recipe will serve 4-6 people. This recipe is also similar to the one used by Jollibee, a Filipino fast-food chain.

Ingredients:

  • Neutral Cooking Oil
  • 4 Hot Dogs (sliced thin)
  • 1 Yellow Onion (diced small)
  • 3 Cloves of Garlic (miced)
  • 1 Pound of Ground Beef
  • Kosher Salt
  • Coarse Ground Pepper
  • 1/3 Cup of Light Brown Sugar
  • 2 Cups of Spaghetti Sauce (can be store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 Cup of Banana Ketchup
  • 1/4 Cup of Tomato Paste
  • 3/4 Cup of Beef Broth
  • 1 Pound of Spaghetti Noodles
  • White Shredded Cheese for Garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat a tablespoon of oil in a pan covered with a lid. Add your hot dogs and cook for 3-4 minutes, then remove afterwards.
  2. Add a small amount of oil and place your onions in the pan. Cook for two minutes and stir in the garlic.
  3. When the pan starts to produce fragrance, add the ground beef and cook until no longer pink. Break apart beef into a mince as it cooks.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Add the brown sugar, banana, tomato paste, and spaghetti sauce and mix very well.
  6. Stir in your beef broth and bring to a low simmer. Then, cover for 15-20 minutes to thicken
  7. Cook your spaghetti noodles to desired tenderness.
  8. Add your hot dogs back into the spaghetti sauce and stir well.
  9. Plate your spaghetti and and top with the sauce.
  10. Sprinkle cheese on top if you like.
  11. Enjoy!
